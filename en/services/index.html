Web design, web development and SEO consultancy services

We like to approach our projects from an integrated perspective, combining an intelligent design with the best web technologies. We also provide specific services, according to our client's needs

Design

Often this is the starting point of our projects. We offer personal, innovative, intelligent and practical solutions. We take care that our designs transfer properly for use in different applications (print or web).

Visual identity

We develop logos and visual identities for all sorts of clients. Once the identity is defined we then apply it to company stationary, the website and any other required applications, creating an intelligently integrated brand identity in a coherent and planned fashion.

Web design

We design the wireframes so our clients can see and understand the web projects before they are online. After thanks to our web engineering services they are developed properly.

Interface design

We design user-centred interfaces for websites and web applications, balancing technical functionality with visual excellence. Our goal is to make user interaction simple, effective and engaging.

Print design

We have a strong background in graphic design for print. We design magazines, catalogues, brochures, marketing packs and any other collateral material. We also take care of any necessary print or production management.

Art direction

Depending on each project's needs, we choose and direct photographers, illustrators, designers and programmers to execute our ideas in both a beautiful and effective way.

Web engineering

The internet has become the ultimate communication and applications platform. Using the most up to date web standards and technologies, we develop your idea, make it solid, scaleable and updatable.

Web development

Guided by web standards and agile development methodologies we build solutions by selecting the programming language and framework that is most suitable for your project requirements, always with scaleability and optimum performance in mind.

Content management Systems (CMS)

We build content management systems so that our clients can edit and update the content on their websites or web applications without the need to have specialist or technical knowledge.

E-commerce

Buying goods and services online is now standard practice. By using an online shop we can create a direct channel of retail communication between your brand and the consumer. In our standard E-Commerce package you can manage all aspects of your online shop, from the products, orders and payments to the postage.

Social Media Integration

Twitter? Facebook? Myspace? We can integrate these services into your existing blog or website or into a new site designed to fit your needs. We can help you to understand and utilise these different branches of new social media in order to intelligently promote your brand or company.

Web Applications

We can build websites to optimise work flow, to automate repetitive processes or to implement a business idea. These websites become online software, helping to streamline your business and raising efficiency.

SEO CONSULTING

Are your clients or potential customers able to find you on Google? We help your site to rank well on all the most used search engines, focusing on your target market and offering realistic solutions.

Keyword analysis

Running a successful SEO campaign depends dramatically on your keywords strategy. We analyse what words or phrases are used by people when they search within your industry and then identify which are most suitable for your business.

Deep site optimisation

You've got your page working online but seems that all the search engines out there do not notice it? We do an On-Page SEO analysis detecting the weak points on your site.

Manual search engine submission

Through manual submission methods we inform the search engines ( Google, Yahoo,etc...) about your site.

Competitor analysis

We can perform a benchmark analysis of how your competitors are currently operating their own SEO strategy. We then guide you to learn form their mistakes, avoid the pitfalls and profit from your competitors weaknesses.
