Thomas Giddings – fashion photographer

Thomas Giddings is a fashion photographer. He has shot for various clients including British and Russian Vogue, i-D, Jalouse, Topman and Mulberry. He balances his commercial work with personal projects and has recently had his second solo photography show in London where he is currently based.

Thomas contacted us because he couldn’t update his old website easily. He also had a blog that was not integrated into his site. We designed and developed a new portfolio website, highlighting his work by utilising the entire width of the screen. We also implemented an easy and engaging navigation system and integrated his blog making it more personal and visually more interesting.

After finishing Thomas’s website we were contacted by other clients from the fashion industry, leading to a new website and an online shop coming soon…

“ I needed a website that was not only easy to navigate but also showcased my photographs and blog in the best way possible. Scroll provided an incredibly professional and dedicated service from the initial design concept to all the final details that make the site unique. ”

THOMAS GIDDINGS
